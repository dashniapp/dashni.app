Dashni Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email, age, gender, location, profile photos and videos when you create an account.

2. How We Use Your Information

To match you with other users, send notifications about matches and messages, and keep the community safe.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your profile is visible to other Dashni users. We use Supabase for database and storage.

4. Data Retention

You can delete your account anytime from Settings. All data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Age Requirement

Dashni is strictly for users 18 and over. Accounts belonging to minors will be immediately terminated.
